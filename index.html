How it works

Send data. Receive clarity.

Four steps from raw spreadsheet to insights in your inbox — most of it runs automatically after the first setup.

01

You upload your data

Drop your Excel, CSV, or Shopify/QuickBooks export into a shared Google Drive folder. That's all you need to do.

02

Automation does the work

Zapier detects the new file, triggers the data pipeline, refreshes your Power BI dashboard, and prepares the report.

03

AI writes the insights

GPT-4 or Claude analyzes your KPIs against targets and prior periods, and generates a plain-English narrative — no jargon.

04

Report lands in your inbox

A formatted PDF report with charts, trend analysis, and recommended actions is emailed to you automatically. Done.
